# Code of Conduct

`__NAME__` is a community-driven project.

- We aim for a welcoming
- professional space where people can build, learn, and collaborate safely.
- This applies to: Issues, Pull Requests, Discussions, and related channels.
- Reference (baseline): https://www.rust-lang.org/policies/code-of-conduct/

---

## Expectations

- ✅ Be respectful; assume good intent
- ✅ Be constructive; critique ideas, not people
- ✅ Be inclusive and considerate
- ✅ Keep discussions on-topic and actionable
- ✅ Respect privacy and consent

## Not acceptable (examples)

- ❌ Harassment, hate speech, threats, or personal attacks
- ❌ Discrimination or exclusionary behavior
- ❌ Sexualized language or unwelcome attention
- ❌ Doxxing or sharing private information
- ❌ Trolling, bad-faith engagement, or deliberate disruption

---

## Reporting

If you experience or witness a violation, report it:

- Private contact via repository channels: [Repository](__REPO_URL__)
- Request a private follow-up via Discussions: **DISCUSSIONS_URL**

For security vulnerabilities, use: **SECURITY_URL**

Include: what happened, where (link), who, and any relevant context. We will handle reports as
confidentially as practical.

---

## Enforcement

Maintainers may take appropriate action, including:

- A request to change tone or content
- Content moderation (edit/remove/lock)
- A formal warning
- Temporary restrictions (mute/limited participation)
- Permanent bans for severe or repeated violations
